About Us

"Krishna Production & Boutique is a curated blend of India’s rich textile legacy and modern fashion sensibilities. We empower skilled regional artisans by bringing their handcrafted sarees and boutique designs to a global audience, one timeless piece at a time.".

Business Plan

Target Audience: Our core audience includes saree enthusiasts, modern women aged 20–50 seeking a blend of tradition and style, as well as members of the Indian diaspora worldwide who wish to stay connected to their roots through cultural fashion..
Monetization:We generate revenue through direct-to-consumer online sales, limited-edition premium collections, and personalized styling consultations. Future offerings include subscription boxes for festive sarees, collaborations with designers, and artisan exclusives.
Vision: Our vision is to build a sustainable digital ecosystem that uplifts rural weaving communities while preserving the soul of Indian textile arts. We aspire to become the go-to platform for authentic, story-rich sarees — bridging tradition and modern fashion for global audiences.
